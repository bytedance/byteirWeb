End-to-end model compilation solution.

ByteIR provides an end-to-end model compilation solution, including frontends, compiler, and runtime. Although all ByteIR components (frontends/compiler/runtime) are together to provide an end-to-end solution and all under the same umbrella, each component is built in a modular way and can perform independently.
